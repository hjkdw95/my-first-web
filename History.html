History of *NSYNC

Chris Kirkpatrick, who had missed the cut for the Backstreet Boys, met with Lou Pearlman in 1995 to talk about forming a second group.[11] Pearlman said that he would finance the group if Kirkpatrick would find other young male singers. Pearlman looked through some tapes they had, and one of Justin Timberlake from The Mickey Mouse Club caught his eye. During the time since The Mickey Mouse Club ended, Timberlake and JC Chasez both made demos together at Timberlake's house in Memphis, Tennessee.[12] Chasez initially left the show when he was seventeen to go to Los Angeles, but felt cheated on after attempting to pursue music there.[12] The two eventually relocated to Nashville with Timberlake's friend while continuously writing songs and demo tracks.
